Daily more than 6.9 million commuters commutes on a daily basis and 
constitutes more than half of the total daily passenger capacity of the Indian Railways itself. 

People buy tickets on counter, or use CVM coupon, there are certain situation when you do not have exact change.
Using this application you can know how much exact change you will require or how many coupon you may need to punch in.

This comes handy when there is large queue at the station.
